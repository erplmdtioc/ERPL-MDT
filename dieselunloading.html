HSD Unloading from Tanker

1. Connect the suction hose to the tank lorry outlet.
2. Ensure proper earthling of tank lorry to avoid charge accumulation.
3. Ensure HOV-5 & 6 in closed condition.
4. Open HOV-1 & tank lorry outlet valve and vent air through needle valve.
5. Ensure HOV-1, 2 & 3 in open condition.
6. Start the Pump motor by green push button.

X

Filling Mainline Engine DST

1. Ensure HOV-1, 2 &3 are in closed condition.
2. Ensure that the Level shown in DST Tank in the SCADA is less than 4 KL and Also ensure level is below high level' in the level indicator tube in the DST..
3. Open HOV-4, 5,6 & 7 with Respective DST inlet valve of MLPU.
4. Ensure HOV-8 is in close condition.
5. Ensure proper venting of pump before starting.
6. Start the pump by green push button.
7. While running the pump, continuous close monitoring to be done by one person so that the DST tank is not filled beyond 4 KL (High level mark in level tube).
8. Pump to be stopped before it reaches 4 KL (high level mark), to avoid overflow.

X

Filling Fire Fighting Engine/ Diesel Generator DST

1. Ensure HOV-1, 2 & 3 are in closed condition.
2. Open HOV-4, 5,6 & 8 with Respective DST inlet valve of DG-1/DG-2/FF-4/FF-5.
3. Monitor the respective DST level for top-up.
4. Ensure proper venting of pump before starting.
5. Start the pump by green push button. While running the pump, continuous close monitoring to be done by one person and fill up to safe filling height of respective DST.
