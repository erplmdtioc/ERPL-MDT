Diesel Unloading from Tanker

1. Position tanker at unloading bay.
2. Connect unloading hose to tanker outlet.
3. Open tanker outlet valve.
4. Open suction valve of unloading pump.
5. Start diesel unloading pump.
6. Monitor level of 80KL tank.
7. Stop pump after unloading complete.

X

Filling Mainline Engine DST

1. Ensure sufficient level in 80KL tank.
2. Open discharge valve towards mainline DST.
3. Start diesel transfer pump.
4. Monitor DST level.
5. Stop pump after required level reached.

X

Filling Fire Fighting Engine DST

1. Check diesel level in storage tank.
2. Open line towards fire engine DST.
3. Start unloading pump.
4. Monitor tank level.
5. Stop pump after filling complete.
